Shakshuka

Description

Shakshouka (Arabic: شكشوكة‎, also spelled shakshuka or chakchouka) is a Maghrebi dish of eggs poached in a sauce of tomatoes, olive oil, peppers, onion and garlic, and commonly spiced with cumin, paprika, cayenne pepper, and nutmeg.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Olive Oil
  • Pepper
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Nutmeg

Directions

  1. Saute onions, bell peppers, and garlic
  2. Add tomatoes to make Shakshouka Sauce
  3. Add eggs
  4. Cook eggs
  5. Garnish and serve
